# Backlog

## Product Spine

Lead → Intake → Case → Diagnostic → Market Review → Branded Deliverable → Follow-up Work

## Phase 1 — Lead Capture

- Contact / intake page
  Capture export-focused lead details and show a confirmation message after submission:
  "We'll review and respond within 1 business day."

- GHL-integrated form
  User will provide the GoHighLevel embed/integration details shortly.
  Replace or augment the first-pass native intake form when details are available.

## Phase 2 — Make the Service Real

- Case / Engagement model
  A case represents a client export situation being analyzed.
  Each case should contain client info, transaction context, diagnostics, market review,
  notes, outputs, and follow-up work.

- Saved diagnostics
  Turn the calculator into saved assessments that can be revisited, compared, and
  attached to a case.

- Market Review builder
  Replace static route-review cards with a structured market review workflow:
  editable fields, internal notes, and client-ready recommendations.

- Branded deliverables
  Produce clean, branded outputs with summary, risks, recommendations, next steps,
  and disclaimers.
