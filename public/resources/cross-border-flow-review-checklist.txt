Dominion Cross-Border Transaction Review Checklist

Use this checklist to gather facts before a professional review.

1. Client and business context
- Client legal name and operating name
- Business purpose for the cross-border flow
- Internal owner for follow-up questions

2. Route facts
- Source country and destination country
- Currency or currencies involved
- Expected frequency and approximate monthly volume
- Known intermediaries or service providers

3. Counterparty facts
- Sender and recipient names
- Relationship between parties
- Available agreements, invoices, or purchase records

4. Timing facts
- Expected dates
- Time-sensitive deadlines
- Reporting or filing dates that may affect review planning

5. Document status
- Records received
- Records pending
- Records requiring specialist review

6. Open questions
- Tax questions
- Legal questions
- Banking or compliance questions
- Operational questions
