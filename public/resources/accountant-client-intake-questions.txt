Dominion Accountant Client Intake Questions

Use these prompts to prepare a neutral intake conversation.

1. What business activity is connected to the cross-border flow?
2. Which countries are involved?
3. Which parties are sending or receiving funds?
4. What is the relationship between the parties?
5. What documents are already available?
6. What documents are still being collected?
7. Is the timing routine, seasonal, or deadline-driven?
8. Are any reporting dates already known?
9. Which internal person owns follow-up?
10. Which questions should be routed to tax, legal, banking, or compliance review?
